7. Color Ratio (Important)
This prevents the design from becoming chaotic.
Recommended ratio:
60% white / light gray
25% navy text and structure
10% teal actions
3% green progress
2% orange rewards

Purple should stay rare, only in gradients.

8. Accessibility

Minimum 4.5:1 contrast ratio.

9. Icon System
Icons should follow the color logic:
Health → Green
Engagement → Teal
Rewards → Orange
Insights → Navy
This helps users understand features quickly.

10. Brand Feeling
Your palette creates a specific emotional tone:
Navy → enterprise trust
Green → health outcomes
Teal → collaboration
Orange → motivation
Purple gradient → modern tech energy
This is very appropriate for a corporate wellness startup.

Why This Is Actually a Strong Product
You combine four retention drivers:
Daily check-in → habit
Promo hour → reward anticipation
Feed → social engagement
Challenges → competition
Most wellness apps only have one or two.
You already have four.

A strong onboarding flow is critical for Seegla because employees will only keep using the app if the first experience is fast, rewarding, and clear. The goal is to get them to their first reward within the first 60 seconds.
Below is the ideal onboarding structure for Seegla.
